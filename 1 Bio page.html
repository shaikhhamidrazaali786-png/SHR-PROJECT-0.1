HRITHIK ROSHAN

Biography

Hrithik Roshan is an Indian actor known for his work in Bollywood films. He was born on January 10, 1974, in Mumbai, India. Hrithik is the son of famous filmmaker Rakesh Roshan and made his acting debut in the film "Kaho Naa... Pyaar Hai" in 2000, which was a massive success and earned him several awards.
